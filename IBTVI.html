Is Better To Visualize It

IBTVI is a software designed to allow the visualization of plane partitions. In addition to the possibility of viewing the plane partition associated with a monomial ideal and the related minimal system of generators, it is possible to perform some basic operations between monomial ideals.
Currently the operations of sum product and intersection between monomial ideals have been implemented. Furthermore, starting from a plane partition associated with a monomial ideal I, it is possible to remove a generator from I and display the new plane partition, or remove a box (add a generator).
