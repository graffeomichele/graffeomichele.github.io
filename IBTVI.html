It's Better To Visualize It

It's Better To Visualize It ( ) is a software designed to allow the visualization of plane partitions. In addition to the possibility of viewing the plane partition associated with a monomial ideal and the related minimal system of generators, it is possible to perform some basic operations between monomial ideals. Currently the operations of sum product and intersection between monomial ideals have been implemented.
IBTVI gives the possibility to edit a monomial ideal in three variables, directly from its associated plane partition, via simple clicks.
A save/load function is also implemented. This allows to save up to 30 plane partitions and reload them at any time.
